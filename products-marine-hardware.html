Technical Specifications

Mirror polish (Ra 0.4μm) standard. Satin, brushed, and electro-polished finishes available on request.
Testing & Certification
Salt spray tested 500+ hours. Material certificates provided. CE compliant. Custom testing per buyer spec.
Customization
Custom sizes, logo engraving, modified designs. Send your drawing or sample for DFM review and quotation.
