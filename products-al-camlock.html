Aluminium Camlock Coupling for Fire Hose

The aluminium camlock coupling is a quick-connect hose fitting for efficient and secure hose connections in fluid transfer systems. Lightweight construction. Commonly used in fire hose systems and industrial fluid transfer applications. Available in 1.5" to 4".

Frequently Asked Questions

What camlock types are available?
All standard types: A, B, C, D, E, F, DC, DP. Available in sizes 1/2" to 6".
What standards do your couplings meet?
MIL-A-A-59326 (US military) and EN 14420-7 (European). Interchangeable with all standard camlocks.
